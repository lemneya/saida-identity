## Using Google NotebookLM to Generate a Global Phenomenon

---

## What is NotebookLM?

Google NotebookLM is an AI tool that can take documents and generate engaging podcast-style audio discussions about them. Two AI hosts discuss the content in a natural, conversational way that makes complex topics accessible and engaging.

Link: https://notebooklm.google.com

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access NotebookLM

1. Go to https://notebooklm.google.com

2. Sign in with your Google account

3. Click "New Notebook"

### Step 2: Upload the Source Document

Upload the file: NOTEBOOKLM_PODCAST_SOURCE.md

This document has been specifically formatted for podcast generation:

- Clear narrative structure

- Dramatic tension (the Moltbook crisis vs. the Ihsan response)

- Emotional moments (the fear, the discovery, the decision)

- Quotable passages

- A clear call to action

### Step 3: Generate the Audio Overview

1. Once the document is uploaded, look for "Audio Overview" in the notebook

2. Click "Generate" to create the podcast

3. Wait for processing (usually 2-5 minutes)

### Step 4: Download and Share

1. Listen to the generated podcast

2. Download the audio file

3. Share across platforms
